Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The Communications Express Reporting Tool is an internal-use application developed by Communications Express LLC to support accounting, reporting, and operational workflows.

Information We Access

With user authorization, the Communications Express Reporting Tool may access data from QuickBooks Online, including company information, customers, vendors, invoices, bills, transactions, and related accounting records.

How We Use Information

Accessed data is used solely for internal business purposes, including reporting, automation, reconciliation, and operational analysis. Data is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing.

Data Storage and Security

Authorized data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized internal users of Communications Express LLC.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained only as long as necessary for internal business purposes. Access to QuickBooks Online data can be revoked at any time through QuickBooks Online.
