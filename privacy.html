Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The Communications Express Reporting Tool is an internal-use application developed by Communications Express LLC to support accounting, reporting, and operational workflows.

Information We Access

With explicit user authorization via Intuit OAuth 2.0, the Communications Express Reporting Tool may access data from QuickBooks Online, including company information, customers, vendors, invoices, bills, transactions, and related accounting records.

The application requests only the minimum scopes necessary to perform its intended internal reporting and automation functions.

Authentication and Authorization

The application uses Intuit’s OAuth 2.0 authorization framework. User QuickBooks credentials are never collected, stored, or transmitted by Communications Express LLC.

Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored securely and are used solely to access authorized QuickBooks Online data on behalf of authorized users.

How We Use Information

Accessed data is used exclusively for internal business purposes, including reporting, reconciliation, automation, and operational analysis. Data is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising or marketing.

Data Transmission and Security

All data transmissions between the application and Intuit services occur over encrypted HTTPS (TLS) connections. Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards are used to protect authorized data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Data Retention

Authorized QuickBooks Online data is retained only for as long as necessary to support internal business operations. Data retention practices are reviewed periodically to ensure alignment with operational requirements.

Revocation and Disconnection

Users may revoke the application’s access to QuickBooks Online at any time through their QuickBooks Online account settings. Upon revocation, the application will no longer be able to access QuickBooks Online data.

Data Deletion Requests

Requests related to data access or deletion may be directed to the contact listed below. Reasonable efforts will be made to respond to such requests in a timely manner.

Contact
